Website Concept: Student Time Management Platform
1. Core Purpose

A web application designed to help students manage tasks, track study sessions, set goals, prepare for exams, and engage in social/competitive learning with friends, all in a visually simple and user-friendly interface.

2. Key Features
2.1 Task and Assignment Management

Create, edit, and delete tasks and assignments.

Assign priority levels (high, medium, low).

Organize tasks by class, project, or subject.

Track deadlines and completion status.

2.2 Calendar

Integrated calendar view (daily, weekly, monthly).

Display tasks, assignments, study sessions, and exam dates.

Notifications and reminders for upcoming deadlines.

Drag-and-drop scheduling for flexibility.

2.3 Goal Setting

Set long-term and short-term goals.

Track progress visually through graphs or progress bars.

Goals linked to tasks and assignments for automatic tracking.

2.4 Study Session Management

Pomodoro Timer: Configurable focus and break sessions.

Gamified Motivation: Visual representations of progress:

Hatch Egg: Progress hatches an egg as tasks are completed.

Ice Melting: Ice melts gradually as study time progresses.

Track completed study sessions with timestamps.

2.5 Analytics

Time spent per subject or task type.

Daily/weekly/monthly completion rates.

Visualization of productivity trends (graphs, charts).

Insights into peak productivity hours.

2.6 Exam Management

Schedule upcoming exams.

Associate exam-related tasks or study sessions.

Track preparation progress.

2.7 Social & Profile Features

Personal profile with statistics and achievements.

Add friends and track their progress.

Competition: Friendly competitions with friends (e.g., who completes more study sessions or tasks).

Leaderboards for motivation.

2.8 Integrations

Google Classroom integration for importing assignments and deadlines automatically
